Thanks for stopping by! I am a postdoctoral fellow in the Chemical Sciences Division at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, in Berkeley, CA. My current research encompasses the fundamental coordination chemistry of the lanthanides and actinides, design and synthesis of improved chelators, and protein conjugation methods for targeted drug delivery.

See more details on my current and past projects.

I also like to develop code in my spare time, and have a working knowledge of scripting the UNIX shell, R, python, and MATLAB. I enjoy tackling complex data analysis problems, writing useful and efficient code, and sharing it. I support open and transparent science, and making data and analysis available to all!
