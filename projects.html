Targeted Alpha Therapy and PET Imaging

Synthesis of biopolymers for chelation and conjugation of alpha-emitting radionuclides to monoclonal antibodies and other targeting vehicles. Characterization of the radioimmunoconjugates and evaluation of their stability, pharmacokinetics, and in vitro and in vivo tumoricidal efficacy. Incorporation of positron-emitting isotopes for imaging and theranostics

http://dx.doi.org/10.1002/9781119115557.ch9




Gadolinium Toxicity and Chelation Treatment

Biodistriubtion and speciation studies of the fate of gadolinium in vivo following administration of MRI contrast agents. Development of new molecules and methods with improved selective chelation for the removal of free gadolinium from the body

http://dx.doi.org/10.1038/s41598-018-22511-6

Vanadium-Dependent Nitrogenase
